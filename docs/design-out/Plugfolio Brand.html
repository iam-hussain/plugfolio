02
The symbol
A two-prong plug whose prongs rise into upward arrows — plug meets growth. Pictorial, monochrome-safe, legible to 16px. No letter.
Exploration → decision
tag notch
drop body
spark point
✓
arrow prongs
Chosen: prongs as upward arrows. It says sales — up and to the right — without adding a second idea. The mark still flattens to one silhouette and survives 16px. The rejected routes each added a detail that muddied the outline or read as a hole at small sizes.
Construction — 100×100 grid

Anatomy
Arrow prongs — two pins whose caps rise to a soft point: the growth cue. Color follows the background (see rule below).
Body — a squared, small-radius brick that sits evenly inside any app tile.
Prongs and body read as one continuous silhouette — the mark flattens to a single shape in one color.
Locked rule — prong & spark color
On light / white
Violet body, ink prongs #12101C. Wordmark spark in violet (or ink) — never lime.
On dark / violet
White (or violet) body, lime prongs and a lime spark. This is where lime is meant to pop.
One exception: under 24px the mark collapses to a single flat color for legibility.
